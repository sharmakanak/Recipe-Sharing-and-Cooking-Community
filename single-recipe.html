Banana Pancakes

Sift together 220 grams of flour, 2 teaspoons of baking powder, and 0.5 teaspoon of baking soda. ... Make a well in the flour mixture, and add the milk and eggs. ... Heat a pan over medium heat. ... Once small bubbles form at the center of the pancake, and the edges are brown- flip the pancake.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Mash and Mix: Mash a ripe banana in a bowl.Add 1 cup of pancake mix and 3/4 cup of milk. Mix until just combined.

step 2

Preheat and Pour: Preheat a griddle or non stick pan over medium heat.Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle.

step 3

Cook and Enjoy: Cook until bubbles from on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.Serve warm and enjoy your quick and tasty banana pancakes.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
